# Garraf & the Litoral

A one-day motorcycle roadbook out of Barcelona — **Sunday 6 September 2026**, 11:30 until dinner.

Open `index.html` in a browser. No build step, no dependencies.

## The day

| Time | Stop | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 11:30 | El Cullerot de Sants | *Esmorzar de forquilla* — the Catalan fork-and-knife breakfast |
| 13:40 | C-31 Les Costes del Garraf | The cliff road, 86 corners above the sea |
| 14:05 | Mirador de Cala Morisca | The one viewpoint on the road with real parking |
| 14:25 | El Chiringuito de Garraf | Coffee on the sand, moto parking by the tunnel |
| 15:10 | Sitges back streets | Park and walk — Sunday trading exemption |
| 16:20 | Sakya Tashi Ling | Buddhist monastery in a modernist mansion, inside the Garraf park |
| 17:20 | BV-2411 over the massif | The road Barcelona riders use to get home |
| 18:55 | Jon Cake, Poblenou | The cheesecake |
| 19:40 | Avinguda del Litoral → Fòrum | The seafront run, golden hour, sunset 20:20 |
| 20:45 | Els Pescadors | Dinner with friends on Plaça de Prim |

~150 km. Every stop verified open on a Sunday.

## Design notes

Roadbook aesthetic: asphalt and ember. Syne for display, Chivo for body, Chivo Mono for
timings and road numbers. The timeline rail is a dashed road centre-line. Mobile-first —
this gets read on a phone at a petrol station. `@media print` collapses it to something you
can fold into a tank bag.

## What was verified, and what wasn't

Confirmed against primary sources:

- **Els Pescadors** — Sunday dinner 20:00–00:15, kitchen 20:00–22:15 (own site)
- **Barcelona ZBE** — motorcycle restrictions are pre-Euro 2 only, enforced Mon–Fri 07:00–20:00 (zbe.barcelona). Sundays are unaffected.
- **Carretera de les Aigües** — closed to motor vehicles, Collserola park ordinance art. 14
- **C-31** — among Catalonia's ten most dangerous roads per RACC; motorcyclists are the majority of serious casualties
- **Turó de l'Home** — final 1.5 km closed to motor traffic (which is why Montseny isn't in this route)

Call before you go:

- **Sakya Tashi Ling** (+34 93 743 20 10) — the monastery's own site and the Catalan press
  publish different Sunday hours, and it closes for retreats without notice.
- **Jon Cake Poblenou** — the 08:00–20:00 daily hours come from press coverage of the opening,
  not a live listing. Fallback: the Santa Caterina shop (Sant Pere Més Baix 36), officially
  open daily 10:00–20:00.
- **El Cullerot de Sants** — Sunday 09:00–17:00 is consistent across aggregators but has no
  primary source. Book anyway; it seats almost nobody.

Deliberately excluded after checking: Cal Pep, Bar Cañete, Quimet i Quimet, El Xampanyet,
Bar del Pla, Suculent and La Cova Fumada are all **closed Sunday**. Bodega 1900 has been
permanently closed since 2020.
